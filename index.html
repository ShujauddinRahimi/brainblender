Learn to Learn

In the modern era of the internet, learning has paradoxically become more difficult. Our devices pull our attention away from more meaningful work and lull us into mental fatigue. My name is Shujauddin Rahimi and to combat this, I have compiled my notes on learning based on the various sources I have studied. I hope you find them useful and that they help you rekindle a love for learning.

Why we need to learn how to learn

When I was in school, I was never explicitly taught how to learn. I was simply thrown into the process and I had to guess my way around until I found an unconcious system that worked for me without even knowing it. As I've grown older, I have found resources that explicitly give the tools necessary to create a system that fosters a positive learning experience and makes it all easier. You'll find that simply having a basic understanding of the process will get rid of the mysticism behind "being smart" and learning.
