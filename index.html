Learn to Learn

Are you the kind of person who thinks learning "just isn't your thing"? Chances are, you might just not know the right techniques to learn in the first place. Here I've collected tips and lessons from various resources!

Why we need to learn how to learn

In school, we're rarely ever taught to learn the process of learning. We are simply thrown into the process with the assumption that we already have the tools we need: time management, prioritization, passion, etc. This website aims to serve as a quick hub for learning and resources.
