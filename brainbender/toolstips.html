Tools & Tips

A collection of brief but valuable articles that hit on the most important aspects of learning. The sources for the information are listed in each article.

Tools

Coursera: Learning how to Learn

The site that sparked my interest, Professor Barbara Oakley does a fantastic job of explaining the process of learning and how you can do it better.

"Mastery" by George Leonard

Learning things worth learning is not an easy task. We like to think that it's a linear process, that how much we put in is how much we get out. George Leonard shows us that this isn't exactly the case and shows us what we should expect and aspire for.

"Atomic Habits" by James Clear

We are what we repeatedly do. We are who we think we are. James Clear clears up the process of making and breaking habits and gives us an imperative to be better.

Quizlet

Quizlet and flashcards in general have been very helpful to me. You won't master something unless you repeat it again. And again. And again.


Tips

Sleep

Get some sleep. Then get some more. 7-9 hours and you'll be golden.

Eat

A healthy diet means a healthy brain! Eat some greens, a couple nuts, and leave the cake for birthdays.

Make Time

Learning and multitasking hate each other. If you want to learn something, make time for it and only it.
